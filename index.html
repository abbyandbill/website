Abby and Bill

This blog contains photos of Abby, Bill, and their time together. It was created as a "birthday surprise" for Abby in 2021, and will be updated periodically going forward. They met on Feb 7, 2017 in Ithaca NY while attending grad school at Cornell, and have been happily together since.

Currently, Abby works as a consultant in Washington, DC. In her spare time, she visits alpaca farms, paints, and goes on walks. Bill is writing his PhD dissertation on earthquakes, and prepping for his next life as a algorithmic trader. They post occasionally on social media.
